**Session date:** 2026-05-17

**What happened:**
- v1 was too noisy (pivotBars=5, single filter — fired on every minor wiggle)
- Rebuilt to v2 with three stacked filters for high-conviction signals only

**v2 signal filters (ALL three must pass):**
1. Dominant pivot: `pivotBars=10` (2-week dominant swing window, was 1-week)
2. Strong pivot candle: `close[pivotBars] > open[pivotBars]` AND `close[pivotBars]` in top/bottom 30% of pivot bar's range — tests the PIVOT BAR itself (not the confirmation bar 10 days later)
3. Minimum swing depth: prior swing must be `>= 2.0 × ATR(14)` — filters shallow wiggles

**Key implementation notes:**
- `swingDepthLong`/`swingDepthShort` computed BEFORE signal conditions — avoids circular dependency
- `lastPivotHigh > 0` / `lastPivotLow > 0` guard is critical: prevents spurious large depth before first opposite pivot
- `close > swingLow` / `close < swingHigh` removed — they were always true by definition of the pivot (dead code)
- Signal bubbles gated with `showLabels` for consistency
- "BUY" bubble at `low` (below bar), "SELL SHORT" at `high` (above bar)
- Status label: "BUY SIGNAL" / "SELL SHORT SIGNAL" / "—"

**Known limitations:**
- ATR sampled at confirmation bar not pivot bar (ThinkScript limitation — `rec atrAtPivot` would fix)
- `lastPivotHigh` may reference a non-adjacent prior pivot in strong trends (acceptable trade-off)
